FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Are you a licensed broker?+

Yes. Welcome Property Management is led by a California-licensed real estate broker, representing clients in both acquisitions and dispositions.

How does management experience help with brokerage?+

Because we manage properties every day, our valuations and due diligence reflect true operating costs and long-term performance — not just comparable sales.

Do you work with investors on 1031 exchanges?+

Yes. We help coordinate the timing and strategy of tax-deferred 1031 exchanges alongside your tax and legal advisors.

Can you both sell my property and manage the next one?+

Absolutely. Many clients rely on us end-to-end — representing them on a sale or purchase and then managing the asset afterward.
