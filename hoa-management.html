FAQ

Frequently asked questions

How do you handle association financials?+

We provide detailed monthly financial statements, manage accounts payable and receivable, collect dues, and reconcile accounts — so the board always has a clear, current picture of the association’s finances.

Will our board have a dedicated point of contact?+

Yes. Every association is assigned a named community manager who knows your property, attends meetings, and is directly reachable — not routed through a call center.

How do you control costs and vendors?+

We competitively bid significant work, maintain a network of vetted and insured vendors, and review every invoice against scope before payment.

Can homeowners pay and submit requests online?+

Yes. An online portal gives homeowners 24/7 access to make payments, view documents, and submit maintenance or architectural requests.
